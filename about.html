Our Mission Statement:

To provide cost-effective R&D solutions for science, engineering, and technology

    L-Egant Solutions (LES) provides cost-effective, high-voltage systems, components, and diagnostics for many applications in pulsed-power and plasma-physics research, typically for switching, triggering, charging, and controlling high voltage pulses, etc. Most of our products are standard designs, although custom designs are also available. So, please don't hesitate to ask if you don't see what you need.

    The Second-Harmonic Dispersion Interferometer (SHDI) is our latest offering, which is typically used to measure the density in plasma, gas, and liquids. The instrument is easy to use and robustly stable. It comes in two configurations: one used to measure a 1-dimensional line-of-sight density and another used to measure a 2-dimensional density profile.

    Our product quality is backed by years of experience and iterative development. Over the past decades my research in physics has studied the injection of intense-ion beams into a fusion plasma, fusion in a Dense Z-pinch, fusion in a Magnetic-Field-Reversed Configuration and ion sources of various types, including those for space propulsion. Along the way I co-founded several fusion start-ups, including: Tri Alpha Energy, Inc., Magneto-Inertial Fusion Technologies, Inc., and (most recently) SAFEnergy, Inc.

    It will be a pleasure to provide you with the highest level of support for your research, efficiently, and at low cost. Thank you for your interest in our products.
